if you have create aeronautic: toolgun mod on you can just import it with this
Few things you have to do once you spawn it in
Take item from barrel below it, the controller is for turning on and off the landing gear and entrance by pressing spacebar

(download the world to take a look if confused with steps below)
1. click the 2 valve handle twice until the landing gear retracted
2. break both of the valve and replace it with 2 cardan shafts connected to 2 small cog with metal brackets
3. use the controller and press spacebar once, it will deploy the landing gears and open up the entrance
4. go into entrace there's 10 copycat panel to place, 5 on each side then apply dark metal block texture on them (it's just decoration)
5. go out and use the controller and hit spacebar again, it will close up and retract landing gears
6. place a small cog under it next to a andesite casing covered small cog, in between 2 corner shafts
7. use the controller and hit space bar again to open it up but this time there will be another leg in front middle with sticker it prevent the ornithopter from sliding off
8. go into the ornithopter, right click the computer to bring up its ui, download the CC Tweaked Code in the same github page and drag the startup.lua inside into the Computer UI to import it
9. either type startup.lua in the computer or reboot the computer or restart the world
10. enjoy your ornithopter :)
